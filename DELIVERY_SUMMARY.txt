                        🎓 CALCULATION DEMONSTRATED
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Example: Building 50 BMS units with 10% wastage

Component: LTC6811-1 (Battery Monitor IC)
- Qty per board: 1
- Alternates: LTC6811-2, LTC6812-1

Inventory:
  LTC6811-1:   50 units
  LTC6811-2:   30 units
  LTC6812-1:   20 units

Calculation:
  Required: 1 × 50 = 50 units
  With wastage (10%): 50 × 1.10 = 55 units
  Total available (all variants): 50 + 30 + 20 = 100 units
  Shortage: MAX(0, 55 - 100) = 0 ✓
